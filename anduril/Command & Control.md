COMMAND AND CONTROL

Lattice for Command & Control (C2) is an AI-powered battle management platform built to accelerate complex kill chains.

Lattice for C2 integrates thousands of sensors and effectors to turn data into decisions at scales and speeds beyond human capacity.

##### Battle Management Platform

<video src="https://cdn.sanity.io/files/61nocsj5/production/da9e6852cbd15147e3e056460a0c8d51e63ce559.mp4" height="646" width="854" controls="">Lattice for Command and Control battle management platform enabling a single operator to supervise hundreds of autonomous systems simultaneously</video>

Lattice for C2 is the human machine interface built to meet the complexity and scale of operating manned, unmanned and autonomous systems. Lattice for C2 simplifies the fleet management of hundreds of systems, fuses thousands of data signals into operator insights, and turns a click of a button into action with machine-to-machine tasking.

Lattice for C2 leverages a distributed mesh network, [Lattice Mesh](https://www.anduril.com/lattice/lattice-mesh), to bring the realities of warfighters at the tactical edge back to the sensors and weapon systems operations centers. Lattice for C2 can be deployed across multiple modalities to meet the environment, ranging from laptops to tablets to operator-worn headsets.

Lattice for C2 integrates legacy and next-generations sensors, effectors, networks, and mission systems in a bidirectional manner with the [Lattice SDK](https://www.anduril.com/lattice/lattice-sdk) to provide an an intelligent, comprehensive, and capable common operating picture in a single pane of glass.

Lattice for C2 leverages frontier AI technologies that enable natural operator interactions and decision aides to make rapid, confident, and effective decisions at machine speed.

## 本地摘录 / 结构化正文

The Integrated Fires Mission Command Project Office under PEO Missiles and Space received the first complete IBCS delivery as part of low-rate initial production on April 22, 2024.

IBCS is described as the cornerstone of air and missile defense modernization efforts, providing transformational mission command with increased battlefield capabilities.

The article states IBCS comprises three major end items: Integrated Collaborative Environment (ICE), Engagement Operation Center (EOC), and Integrated Fire Control Network Relay. The IFCN Relay provides the interface for adapted sensors and weapons and serves as a communications relay to extend battlefield operations distances.

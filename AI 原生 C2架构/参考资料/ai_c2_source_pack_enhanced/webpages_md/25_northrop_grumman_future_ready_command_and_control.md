## 本地摘录 / 结构化正文

Northrop Grumman states that the Integrated Battle Command System modular open architecture ensures integration with current and future defense systems across various domains.

The page describes an adaptive framework and notes that IBCS can remain responsive to dynamic mission requirements, including mobile configurations for expeditionary operations and layered homeland defense.

It also describes the EPIC facility in Madison, Alabama as supporting IBCS full-rate production and future high-rate manufacturing.

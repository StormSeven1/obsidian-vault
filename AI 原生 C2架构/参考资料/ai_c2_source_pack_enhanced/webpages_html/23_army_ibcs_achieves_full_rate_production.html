## 本地摘录 / 结构化正文

The Integrated Fires Mission Command Project Office announced that the Army’s IBCS received a successful full-rate production Defense Acquisition Board decision on April 10.

This milestone followed completion of initial operational test and evaluation in October 2022.

Testing and evaluation proved IBCS capability to integrate a variety of sensors and effectors to work together under an integrated command and control system. The article describes IBCS as a key component of Army modernization efforts and transformational air and missile defense capabilities.
